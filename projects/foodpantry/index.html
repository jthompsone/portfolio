Mission Nutrition

Mission Nutrition DeForest Inc (MND) exists to fight hunger and hardship in DeForest Wisconsin and surrounding communities.

Mission Nutrition DeForest (MND) provides free food and grocery items to individuals in need, with no residential or income restrictions. We Offer:

Drive-thru food distributions and Senior Shopping (60 & older). Guests are welcome to visit as often as needed each month.

We offer various volunteer opportunities and donation options to support our mission.

Our mission is to distribute food and grocery items through community events and meal programs that serve families, children, seniors, and individuals at risk of hunger; so that these families do not go hungry. Our goal is to improve food security among all we serve, educate the public on the hunger problem with our neighbors, and advocate for legislation that protects people from going hungry.
